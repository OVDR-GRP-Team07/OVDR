Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2025

The Online Virtual Dressing Room (OVDR) application respects your privacy. This policy outlines what data we collect and how we use it.

What Data Do We Store?

  • Uploaded user images (used solely for virtual try-on)
  • Clothing selections and saved outfit combinations
  • Email addresses used for sending try-on results are not stored on the server
  • Passwords are stored using secure hashing and never saved in plain text

How Do We Use Your Data?

  • Images are used to generate try-on results and are stored locally under your user ID
  • Outfit combinations help you revisit your favorite looks
  • Emails are used one-time to send try-on images
  • Passwords are used for authentication only

Data Retention

All try-on data remains on our development server unless you request deletion or clear your account.
